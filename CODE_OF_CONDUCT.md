# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for all contributors, regardless of experience level, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, disability, personal appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, age, religion, nationality, or other similar characteristic.

## Our Standards

### Expected Behavior

- **Be respectful** - Treat others with respect and consideration
- **Be constructive** - Provide helpful, actionable feedback
- **Assume good intent** - Begin with the assumption that people mean well
- **Disagree constructively** - It's okay to disagree, but do so professionally
- **Be inclusive** - Welcome newcomers and help them get started
- **Focus on what's best** - Keep the project goals and community in mind

### Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind
- Insulting, derogatory, or offensive comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Trolling, intentionally disruptive behavior
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All project spaces (GitHub issues, PRs, discussions)
- Communication channels (if any are established)
- Project events (virtual or in-person)
- Representing the project in public spaces

## Enforcement

### Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, or have concerns about conduct:

1. **For general issues**: Report to project maintainers via GitHub issue (mark as private if needed)
2. **For sensitive issues**: Contact maintainers directly (email addresses will be provided)
3. **For security issues**: See [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md) for vulnerability reporting

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

### Consequences

Project maintainers have the right and responsibility to:
- Remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that don't align with this Code of Conduct
- Temporarily or permanently ban contributors for behaviors they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful

### Process

1. **First offense** - Warning and clarification of expectations
2. **Second offense** - Temporary suspension from project participation
3. **Third offense or severe violation** - Permanent ban from the project

Severe violations (threats, harassment, hate speech) may result in immediate permanent ban.

If you have questions about this Code of Conduct, please open a discussion or contact the maintainers.
